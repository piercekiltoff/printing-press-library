# PokeAPI Absorb Manifest

Approved novel features:

1. `pokemon profile` — combine core pokemon data, species metadata, type names, abilities, stats, and move counts.
2. `pokemon evolution` — resolve species and evolution chain into readable evolution paths.
3. `pokemon matchups` — summarize weaknesses, resistances, immunities, and offensive coverage.
4. `pokemon moves` — flatten and filter move version-group details.
5. `team coverage` — compound individual type relations into roster-level team coverage.
